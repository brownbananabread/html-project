Our Process

We pride ourselves on a streamlined and simple approach to WHS consultancy to best suit your business requirements and achieving compliance

1
1

Consultation

Stage 1 entails meeting with your key staff to determine the scope and timeline of the project.

This stage enables us to best determine at-risk areas in your business and drive the initial tasks to be completed during the project.

What We Do

  • Meet with key stakeholders
  • Assess current WHS practices
  • Identify at-risk areas
  • Define project scope and timeline
2
2

Implementation

Upon determination of the at-risk areas within your business, we will work in with your managers and workers to implement the newly created procedures.

Our implementation approach is people focused, driving continuous culture improvement and a consultation driven project.

What We Do

  • Develop tailored WHS procedures
  • Collaborate with your team
  • Provide training and support
  • Foster safety culture improvement
3
3

Review

On completion of the project, we will review the effectiveness of the implemented processes to re-assess the status of your organisation's compliance.

This includes recommendations from ESS on driving compliance moving forward upon completion of the initial project.

What We Do

  • Evaluate process effectiveness
  • Reassess compliance status
  • Provide ongoing recommendations
  • Create continuous improvement plan
